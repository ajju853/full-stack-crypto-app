About Asset

Bitcoin is the pioneering digital currency, revolutionizing the concept of money through peer-to-peer technology. It operates without a central bank or authority, allowing for secure and anonymous transactions. Created by the enigmatic Satoshi Nakamoto, its source code is open to the public, inviting collaboration and innovation. As we witness the evolution of finance, Bitcoin offers a decentralized means of exchange, enabling seamless electronic transfers with minimal fees. It empowers users by protecting their identities and operates globally, independent of traditional banking systems. With a capped supply of 21 million BTC, Bitcoin is designed to be a deflationary asset. It employs the SHA-256 hashing algorithm, boasting an average transaction confirmation time of just 10 minutes. Today, miners utilize specialized ASIC chips to secure the network, contributing to a hash rate that has reached peta hashes. As the first successful cryptocurrency, Bitcoin has inspired a wave of alternatives, including Litecoin, Peercoin, and Primecoin. The advent of Ethereum introduced smart contracts, paving the way for groundbreaking projects like EOS, Tron, and even unique digital collectibles such as CryptoKitties.
